About Jokel Fashion World

Jokel Fashion World is more than a name — it’s a movement. Born from passion, shaped by craftsmanship, and powered by vision, Jokel Fashion World blends artistry with education to redefine fashion for a new era.

From the heart of Ghana to the global stage, we train the next generation of visionary designers, stylists, and entrepreneurs. Our mission is to transform creativity into careers and raw ideas into runway realities.

Whether you dream of couture design, pattern mastery, modeling, or fashion business leadership, Jokel Fashion World is where your story begins. We don’t just create fashion — we craft legacies.

Our Vision

To be most recognized hub for fashion excellence, innovation, and sustainable design worldwide — setting trends that echo across continents.

Our Mission

To inspire and empower individuals to master the art of fashion through creativity, education, mentorship, and opportunity.

Our Journey

  • 2015 — The spark ignited. Jokel Fashion began as a small atelier teaching basic tailoring.
  • 2018 — Moving on to greater heights, we expanded into the realm of business
  • 2021 — Our first graduates showcased collections.
  • 2025 — Working on exapnding our school and business to greater heightss
